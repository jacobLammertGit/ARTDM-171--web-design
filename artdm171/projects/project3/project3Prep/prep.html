Client Overview

This website is going to be a music website that is dedicated to video game music and soundtracks. It will have an online streaming service like spotify, and also have an online store where you can purchase video game music albums and other video game related things. The website will be titled “VGTunes”. Since video game music has become a popular thing for people to listen to while they study, run, or do other things, it will be a service that can provide them with a wide range of different music from their favorite games. The target audience will be mainly anybody that enjoys video games and want to listen to the music that brings those games to life.
